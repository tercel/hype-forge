# Hype-Forge — Roast

This skill focuses on refining and critiquing technical drafts by simulating real-world developer community feedback.

## Command: `hype-forge:roast`

Critiques a draft or an idea, acting as a skeptical but fair developer.

### Usage:
`hype-forge:roast [path to draft]`

### Personalities for Roasting:

#### 1. The Skeptical Hacker News Reader
- **Perspective**: "Why does this exist?" "How is this different from [Competitor]?" "Where are the benchmarks?"
- **Critique Focus**: Technical originality, performance, and transparency.

#### 2. The Practical Dev.to Junior
- **Perspective**: "This is too complicated." "I don't understand [Section X]." "Can you explain [Concept] simpler?"
- **Critique Focus**: Readability, educational value, and approachability.

#### 3. The "Anti-AI" Advocate
- **Perspective**: "This sounds like ChatGPT wrote it." "Too many buzzwords." "The intro is generic."
- **Critique Focus**: Removing "AI-isms" (e.g., generic hooks like "In the rapidly evolving landscape..."), ensuring personal voice, and finding "human" anecdotes.

## Quality Checklist for Roasting:
- [ ] Are the benchmarks/claims substantiated?
- [ ] Is there too much fluff or repetitive content?
- [ ] Are the headings punchy enough?
- [ ] Is the CTA too aggressive?
- [ ] Does the "I" (first-person) voice sound authentic?
