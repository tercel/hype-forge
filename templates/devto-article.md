# Dev.to / Hashnode Article Template

Title: "How I Built [Your Tool Name] – A [One-sentence selling point] Using [Tech Stack] in Just [Time]"

Target Audience: Mid-level developers facing [Specific pain point, e.g. slow local dev with large datasets].

## Structure:

- **Hook**: A short personal story or pain point question.
- **Problem**: Why existing solutions (X, Y, Z) didn't work for me.
- **My Approach**: Architecture diagram (text-based or Mermaid) and logic breakdown.
- **Key Code**: 3-5 main snippets with narrative explanations.
- **Live Demo / GIF**: Placeholder for a visual demonstration.
- **Results / Benchmarks**: How it performed or what was achieved.
- **Lessons**: What I'd do differently next time.
- **CTA**: GitHub: [Link] | Demo: [Link] | Feedback welcome in comments!

---
*Style Note: Use "I" a lot, add humor lightly, and keep it engaging.*
